Healthy Citizen Disclaimer

Do not rely on Healthy Citizen to make decisions regarding your medical care. Always share this information with and consult with your healthcare practitioner about the potential risks and benefits of using any FDA-regulated products. Specifically,

  • Patients should never stop or change medication without first consulting with your healthcare practitioner.

  • Health Citizen consolidates information from several trusted government data sources and each data source has its own update frequency. Healthy Citizen provides you the most current data available from each trusted source.

Healthy Citizen delivers you trusted information on the FDA regulated products that you use to enable you to make better and informed decisions about your health in conjunction with your health practitioners.

WHAT ARE HEALTHY CITIZEN WIDGETS?

The vast amount of public information on government websites can be hard to digest. Healthy Citizen widgets were designed to address this challenge. By harnessing the data on government health sites--especially those hosted by FDA—Healthy Citizen gives you current, targeted, and trusted health information on approved FDA-regulated products.

Whether you’re a patient, caregiver, healthcare practitioner, or researcher – Healthy Citizen makes it easier for you to improve health outcomes. With Healthy Citizen, patients and caregivers can access information on recalls, potential negative side effects, and potential drug-to-drug interactions related to approved FDA-regulated products. The widgets can present data in dynamic tables, intuitive graphs and/or interactive visualizations. Patients and caregivers can use this information when speaking with their healthcare practitioner to facilitate the shared decision-making process, leading to more informed health choices..

Healthy Citizen delivers you trusted FDA information on regulated products and supports FDA’s goal of empowering consumers to make better and more informed decisions about their health.

  • WHAT IS A WIDGET?
     

    A widget is a relatively simple and small piece of software code that can be installed into web portal. The Healthy Citizen Widgets pull publicly available FDA information and present the information in an easily understandable manner.

  • HOW DO I GET ACCESS TO THESE WIDGETS?

    Contact your healthcare practitioner or patient advocacy group and ask about adding the Healthy Citizen Widgets to their web portal. This information is valuable to healthcare practitioners and their patients.

Healthy Citizen Widgets Currently Available

Adverse Event

The Adverse Event Widget will display negative side effects for FDA regulated products in a table format.

Recalls

The Recall Widget will display recalls for FDA regulated products in a table format.

Drug Visualization

The Drug Interactions Visualization provides a user with a visual representation of the medications that interact with each other.

Drug Interactions

The Potential Drug Interactions table view provides a user with a tabular list of the medications that interact with each other.

Potential Side Effects

The Potential Drug Interactions table view provides a user with a tabular list of the medications that interact with each other.

NDC Look-Up

The NDC Look-Up Widget allows the user to look-up medications, based upon the medication name or National Drug Code (NDC).

User Preferences

The User Preferences widget allows users to pre-select the lists of drugs they are interested in learning about.

GETTING STARTED

It is easy to integrate the Healthy Citizen Widgets into your existing portal or web solution for use by your patients, clients and/or members. The three steps below outlines what your technical team needs to do to allow your organization to leverage the widgets of chose. When logged into the Widget Manager platform more detailed instructions will be given on how to:

  • Explore the widgets and their capabilities to help you decide which widgets are the best fit for your organization and user population(s).
  • Configuring the widgets for your organization.
  • Install the required code snippets into your site to finish the implementation.

Once this is complete, your users will be able to access the trusted, timely, and personalized health data that the Healthy Citizen Widgets provide.

  • 01. Register

    Create your secure account on http://widget-manager.healthycitizen.org - one centralized place where you can manage all your Healthy Citizen Widgets.

  • 02. Configure

    Configure your widget presentation, including the type, color schema, and FHIR Integration parameters.

  • 03. Embed

    Grab the widget code snippet and paste it on your website for your existing users to benefit from being up-to-date with the tailored latest FDA information.
